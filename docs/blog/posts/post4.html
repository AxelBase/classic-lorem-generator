Sharing Lorem Ipsum: The Power of URL Parameters

Published: November 26, 2025

You finally nailed the perfect mockup text: 8 medium paragraphs, real first paragraph enabled, long length. Your client loves it.

Then someone asks: “Can you send me that exact version?”

Before this tool: you’d screenshot, export, or try to remember the settings.

Now? Just click **Share URL**.

How It Works

Every setting is encoded directly into the URL using clean query parameters:

  • ?p=8 → 8 paragraphs
  • &len=medium → medium length
  • &realFirst=true → classic first paragraph

When anyone opens that link, they see exactly what you saw — same count, same length, same text. No explanation needed.

Real-World Use Cases

  • Send mockup-ready text to developers
  • Share approved placeholder blocks with copywriters
  • Link directly from Figma comments
  • Bookmark your favorite configurations
  • Teach students consistent placeholder standards

Because it’s 100% static and client-side, the shared link works instantly — no login, no tracking, no delay.

Pro Tip

Combine with browser bookmarks: save your most-used configurations (e.g., “Blog Post Mockup”, “Landing Page Hero”, “Email Template”) and access them with one click.

Great teams don’t guess at placeholder text — they share it perfectly, every time.
