Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 24, 2025 • Last Updated: November 24, 2025

At Classic Paragraph Lorem Generator (the “Tool”), your privacy is not just respected — it is guaranteed by design.

1. We Collect Nothing

This tool runs 100% in your browser. When you generate Lorem Ipsum text:

  • No text is ever sent to any server
  • No IP addresses are logged
  • No cookies are set
  • No analytics, tracking pixels, or fingerprinting are used

Your generated paragraphs exist only in your browser’s memory and disappear when you close the tab.

2. How the Tool Works (Client-Side Only)

All logic — from paragraph selection to word trimming and URL parameter handling — happens locally using JavaScript. The classic Latin text is bundled directly into the site. Even the Bootstrap framework is loaded from a CDN but does not track you.

3. Offline-First = Maximum Privacy

After your first visit, the entire tool is cached by your browser. You can use it on a plane, train, or submarine with zero internet — and zero privacy risk.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Meta Pixel
  • Advertising networks
  • Session recording tools

The only external resources are Bootstrap 5 and Bootstrap Icons from jsDelivr — both privacy-respecting CDNs with no tracking.

5. Children’s Privacy

This tool is safe and appropriate for users of all ages. Since no data is collected, we fully comply with COPPA and similar regulations by default.

6. International Compliance

Because we collect no personal data, the Tool inherently complies with GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, and all other privacy laws — no action required.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our approach (highly unlikely), we will update this page and the effective date above.

8. Contact

This tool is proudly developed and maintained by AxelBase. Questions? Open an issue on our public GitHub repository.

We believe privacy isn’t a feature — it’s the foundation. That’s why this tool will never collect your data.
